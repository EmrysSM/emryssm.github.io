Intro

Work

My current role is as a lead technical specialist for GRASP at the CDC. My responsibilities include GIS development, ArcGIS enterprise administration, and system design and project architecting, and the occasional big data analytics. Previously, I worked for a transportation engineering firm as a senior developer, led a team of software engineers, worked directly with clients, and participated in sales pursuits as the technical lead. With a focus on custom web applications, I have lead the production of porducts that range from internal business and project mamagement tools to websites and surveying apps for public use. I have interfaced with third party systems that include ESRI products like WebApp Builder, and ArcGIS Enterprise. Along with my experience in developing custom applications I have developed a felxibility to work with many technologies and tech stacks. Languages I am comfortable in are primarily JavaScript and Python. I have used them both in a backend capacity with Node and Django, mated with either a SQL or NoSQL database. Serving out APIs in ways such as REST or GraphQL, my frontend experience primarily includes REACT and Vue. Before software engineering I started my career by providing server and networking services to local businesses, which gave me a strong foundation in systems, architecture, working with clients and meeting their business needs. I graduated from Georgia State University with a BS in Computer Science.
