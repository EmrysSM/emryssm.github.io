Work

I am an application developer and team lead for a national engineering firm in the transportation sector. I have been most recently working as a developer in a GIS team for the past two years. My work, and the growth of my projects, has lead to the creation of a dedicated development team. With a focus of custom web applications, I have had projects that include using off the shelf ESRI products like ArcMap, Survey123, WebApp Builder, ArcGIS Server, ArcGIS Portal, ArcGIS Online, Collector. I have also developed custom applications using Python, ArcGIS JavaScript API, RESTful services, Leaflet, and GeoJSON. I also work with some internal business applications hosted on AWS using Elastic Beanstalk for deployment. These are primarily developed on Django, using python, HTML, JS, CSS, bootstrap, JQuery, and PostgreSQL.
